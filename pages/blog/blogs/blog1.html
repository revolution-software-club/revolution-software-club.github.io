Neuromorphic Computing

Written by Aakanksha Darekar

It is the method of computer engineering in which elements of a computer are customized after the system in the human brain and nervous system. It is also called as neuromorphic engineering.

NEUROMORPHIC TECHNOLOGY CAN BE USED IN THE EXPECTED WAYS:-

  • Autonomous systems, such as robotics, drones, self-driving cars and artificial intelligence.
  • Deep learning applications.

HOW DOES THIS WORK?

  • Neuromorphic computing begins with installation of Artificial Neural Networks(ANN) which consists of millions of neurons.
  • The most common form of neuromorphic hardware is the spiking neural network (SNN). In this hardware, nodes -- or spiking neurons -- process and hold data like biological neurons.Artificial synaptic devices connect spiking neurons. These devices use analog circuitry to transfer electrical signals that mimic brain signals.

FEATURES OF NEUROMORPHIC COMPUTING:-

  • Rapid response system
  • Low consumption of power
  • Mobile Architecture
  • Fast-paced learning
  • High adaptability and plasticity
CHALLENGES:-
  • Accuracy
  • limited software and algorithm
  • Inaccessible
  • Benchmarks
