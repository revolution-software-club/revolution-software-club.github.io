Tech Department

The tech department, short for technology department, is a crucial component of a club responsible for managing and implementing technological solutions. This department is tasked with developing, maintaining, and supporting the club as well university with their innovative real-life projects, software applications, and hardware systems. It plays a key role in ensuring the smooth operation of technology-related processes, cybersecurity, and innovation initiatives. The tech department collaborates with other departments to align technology, enhance efficiency, and stay abreast of emerging trends to maintain a competitive edge.
